## Creating Your Own Videos

### Equipment Needed:
- **Smartphone** (iPhone/Android with good camera)
- **Gimbal/Stabilizer** (optional, for smooth footage)
- **Drone** (optional, for aerial shots)

### Video Ideas for Your Business:

#### 1. **Project Time-Lapse** (30-60 seconds)
```
Setup:
- Mount camera in fixed position
- Take photos every 5-10 minutes
- Compile into time-lapse video
- Add your logo and music

Content:
- Installation process from start to finish
- Shows professionalism and efficiency
- Demonstrates scale of projects
```

#### 2. **Showcase Reel** (45-60 seconds)
```
Structure:
0:00-0:10 - Company logo and tagline
0:10-0:20 - Completed project #1 (exterior)
0:20-0:30 - Completed project #2 (detail shot)
0:30-0:40 - Completed project #3 (interior)
0:40-0:50 - Team at work
0:50-1:00 - Contact information and CTA

Tips:
- Use smooth transitions
- Add upbeat background music
- Include text overlays with project names
```

#### 3. **Before & After** (30 seconds)
```
Structure:
0:00-0:15 - "Before" footage (slow pan)
0:15-0:30 - "After" footage (same angle)

Effect:
- Shows transformation
- Highlights your impact
- Very engaging for viewers
```

#### 4. **Team Introduction** (45 seconds)
```
Content:
- Quick shots of team members at work
- Professional equipment and tools
- Quality materials being installed
- Satisfied clients (with permission)

Message:
- "Meet the AluMaster team"
- "Quality craftsmanship"
- "Professional service"
```

### Filming Tips:

#### Camera Settings:
- **Resolution**: 1080p minimum (4K preferred)
- **Frame Rate**: 30fps or 60fps
- **Orientation**: Landscape (horizontal)
- **Lighting**: Natural daylight is best

#### Composition:
- **Rule of Thirds**: Place subjects off-center
- **Stable Shots**: Use tripod or stabilizer
- **Variety**: Mix wide shots, medium shots, close-ups
- **Movement**: Slow pans and tilts only

#### Editing:
- **Length**: Keep it short (30-60 seconds)
- **Music**: Use royalty-free music
- **Text**: Add company name and tagline
- **Logo**: Include your logo (watermark)

## Uploading to YouTube

### Step-by-Step:

1. **Create YouTube Channel**
   - Go to youtube.com
   - Click "Create a channel"
   - Name it "AluMaster Aluminum System"

2. **Upload Video**
   - Click "Create" → "Upload video"
   - Select your video file
   - Add title: "AluMaster - Professional Aluminum & Glass Solutions"
   - Add description with keywords
   - Set visibility to "Public" or "Unlisted"

3. **Optimize for Embedding**
   - Enable embedding in video settings
   - Allow autoplay
   - Disable ads (if possible)

4. **Get Video URL**
   - Copy the URL from address bar
   - Format: `https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VIDEO_ID`

5. **Add to Website**
   - Paste URL in admin panel
   - Select "YouTube" as video type
   - Enable autoplay
   - Save changes

## Vimeo Alternative

### Why Use Vimeo:
- ✅ No ads on videos
- ✅ Better video quality
- ✅ More professional appearance
- ✅ Privacy controls
- ❌ Free plan has upload limits

### Vimeo Setup:
1. Create account at vimeo.com
2. Upload video
3. Set privacy to "Public" or "Unlisted"
4. Copy video URL
5. Paste in admin panel
6. Select "Vimeo" as video type

## Video Specifications

### Recommended Settings:

| Setting | Value | Reason |
|---------|-------|--------|
| **Resolution** | 1920x1080 (1080p) | HD quality, good file size |
| **Aspect Ratio** | 16:9 | Standard widescreen |
| **Frame Rate** | 30fps or 60fps | Smooth playback |
| **Duration** | 30-60 seconds | Keeps attention |
| **File Format** | MP4 (H.264) | Universal compatibility |
| **Audio** | AAC, 128kbps | Clear sound |
| **File Size** | Under 500MB | Faster upload |

### Mobile Optimization:
- Test on mobile devices
- Ensure text is readable
- Check loading speed
- Verify autoplay works

## Testing Checklist

Before going live with your video:

- [ ] Video plays correctly on desktop
- [ ] Video plays correctly on mobile
- [ ] Autoplay works (muted)
- [ ] Video quality is HD (1080p+)
- [ ] Text overlay is readable
- [ ] Loading time is acceptable
- [ ] Fallback image works when video disabled
- [ ] Video is relevant to your business
- [ ] No copyright issues with content/music
- [ ] Company branding is visible
